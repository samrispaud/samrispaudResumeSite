The Schizophrenia Cognition Lab at Wesleyan University is headed by Dr. Matthew Kurtz. The lab seeks to understand schizophrenia as a disorder of neurocognition, and then elucidate the implications of this understanding for rehabilitation. Studies by the lab are focused on the development and evaluation of novel behavioral approaches for treatment.
